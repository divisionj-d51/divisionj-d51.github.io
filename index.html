Become a confident speaker and leader with Division J.

Division J is a group of Toastmasters clubs in Malaysia, part of Toastmasters District 51. Our clubs help professionals, students, and community members practice public speaking and leadership in a supportive, structured environment — and our free Division J ELEVATE program adds career-focused leadership training powered by AI tools.

Questions people ask us

Straight answers first. The full list lives on our FAQ page.

Where can I learn public speaking in Malaysia?

Toastmasters clubs in Division J of District 51 offer structured, practice-based public speaking training across [EDIT: locations]. Guests attend free, and members learn by giving real speeches with constructive evaluation at every meeting.

Is Toastmasters worth it compared to a paid course?

Toastmasters costs a fraction of commercial courses and runs year-round rather than for a single weekend. Instead of watching an instructor, you speak at every meeting and receive structured feedback — skill grows through repetition, not lectures.

What is Division J ELEVATE?

ELEVATE is Division J's free leadership development series: 22 online sessions from August 2026 to June 2027, each built on practices from named corporate programs and integrating AI tools such as Claude, Perplexity, and Notion AI into practical career skills.
